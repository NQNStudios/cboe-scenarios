Getting Started
Welcome to Echoes: Assault!

In the scenario folder, you will find a few items of interest. They are:

  • assault.exs

    This is the scenario file itself. Mac users should place it in the Blades of Exile Scenarios folder. C users should place it in the bladscen folder.

  • assault.meg

    This is the graphics file for Mac users. Place it in the Blades of Exile Scenarios folder.

  • assault.bmp

    This is the graphics file for PCs. Place it in your bladscen folder.

  • BOSTON.SAV

    This is the default and heavilly reccomended prefab party for PC players. It has all statistics and items already placed.

  • ChronoReaper.mid

    This is the music file for Echoes: Assault. It is reccomended, though not required, for gameplay.

    This is the .sit file with the default and heavilly reccomended prefab party for Mac players. It has all statistics and items already placed.

Walkthrough
Due to the unique action-based nature or Echoes: Assault, similar to An Apology in a few respects, the scenario is based more around going through tight situations and you are pretty much held by the hand as you go through. The parts you might need help on, however, are listed below:

  • When defending Earthford, take out the cannons, dragons, and Passive Illithids. In the first wave, the rest will retreat after you take out the cannons and Passive Illithid.
  • Never forget your special spells! There are many places where you lose, die, or are stuck for a while when you don't cast your spells. Most of them are necessary throughout the game, one of them can make things easier in a boss battle when used.
  • This scenario can be finished, but it can also be won. Both are difficult, and in fact finishing might be more difficult in some aspects, but search each area you encounter carefully, especially when there doesn't seem to be any pressing force making you go places.
  • Search around for treasure. In many places, there is a spellbook or two, or perhaps some potions. All are quite valuable, considering the difficulty of this scenario.
    On a final note, I wish you the best of luck. It'll be needed.
