The Story

You have heard about how a few years ago, a group of Exile adventurers snuck up to the surface and eliminated the six plagues. Everybody thought it was safe.


It wasn't.


The last plague has appeared on the surface. You are a band of adventurers from the continent of Aizo. You had heard of the plague, but you had no idea they were here.


You have just arrived at Fort Korin. When you arrived a soldier said you should see Commander Jason, he is looking for some adventurers to undertake a mission for him.


Now, there is nothing to do but adventure.......


The Seventh Plague is my first adventure, about a new plague of strange, armored creatures called the Fleshes, terrorizing a small island to the south of Aizo called Indra Island. You are a group of adventurers from Aizo, and decided to take a small vacation. You came here, expecting to find a good place to relax, but end up getting mixed up in another mess.


You have to travel across the huge Indra Island to get to the many towns and dungeons, and landmarks such as Blackfire Volcano. You have to fight your way through new and old places alike. You have to fight to defeat The Seventh Plague, and save Indra Island, or the creatures will spread, and all will be lost.
