How to make April

This is a step by step guide on how to make the character April for my scenario, Dirty Gold. Why don't I just include the party file? Because 90 percent of the time, it gets corrupted. I've made this guide as easy as possible, so you'll have April up and running in no time.

 

STEP 1: Create a new party in Blades of exile. Leave it just as it is, and save it.

STEP 2: Open the party in the BoE editor. Delete everyone's items.

STEP 3: Edit Jenneke's traits. Take away Magically Inept and add Toughness.

STEP 4: Edit Jenneke's statistics. Raise health to 30, raise intelligence to 5, and raise pole weapon skill to 6.

STEP 5: Change the parties gold to 0.

STEP 6: Save the party, leave the party editor, and enter the scenario Dirty Gold with the party.

STEP 7: Delete all other party members EXCEPT Jenneke.

STEP 8: Change Jenneke's name to April, and give her the female with a lyre (harp) graphic.
