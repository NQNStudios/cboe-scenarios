How to make Granson

 

This is a step by step guide on how to make the character Granson for my scenario, Chains. Why don't I just include the party file? Because 90 percent of the time, it gets corrupted. I've made this guide as easy as possible, so you'll have granson up and running in no time.

 

STEP 1: Create a new party in Blades of exile. Leave it just as it is, and save it.

STEP 2: Open the party in the BoE editor. Delete everyone's items EXCEPT Jenneke's.

STEP 3: Edit Jenneke's traits. Take away Magically Inept and add Magically Apt and Toughness.

STEP 4: Edit Jenneke's statistics. Raise health AND spell points to 30, raise intelligence to 5, and give 3 points of mage AND priest skill.

STEP 5: Give Jenneke the following items: Pants, Torches, Iron Broadsword, Robes, Boots, and Bronze Serpent Ring.

STEP 6: Change the parties gold to 167, and their food to 98.

STEP 7: Save the party, leave the party editor, and enter the scenario Chains with the party.

STEP 8: Delete all other party members EXCEPT Jenneke.

STEP 9: Change Jenneke's name to Granson, and give him the male fighter/magic user graphic of your choice.*

 

*You could make Granson a girl, if you pretended she had parents who liked... odd names. :)
