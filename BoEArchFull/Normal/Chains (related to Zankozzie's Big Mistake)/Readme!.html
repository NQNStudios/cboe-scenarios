Thanks for downloading

Chains

An historical biography by Ryan Phelps

Questions? Comments? Criticisms? Email me at ryan@dougphelps.com

 

Chains is an historical biography about Linda, the character in the Exile series. Those who played the series will know her as the ruination of the Tower of Magi, through her experiments with demons. Rumor has it she even summoned the Arch demon. The question is: Why? What drove her to do such dangerous experiments? The answers just might lie in her past, before her exile...

Chains is made for a one PC party. Specifically Granson, the star of my last scenario, Zankozzie's Big Mistake. Chains is, in a sense, a sequel. I didn't include the party Granson, as it always becomes corrupted. Instead, I included a step by step instruction sheet entitled "How To Make Granson." Please open this up and use it before you start my scenario. It is important that you use the party Granson, as Chains has been hand tailored for him, and you could get stuck using a higher level party.

Also, please make sure the graphics work properly. If you can see a little girl graphic in the eastern part of the town you start in, it is working. Otherwise please exit and fix the problem. Mac users will have to convert the gif to an meg, as they too become corrupted when compressed in zip format. It is quite simple to do: just make a copy of another graphics sheet (such as Valley of Dying Things) change the name to past.meg, open it up in ResEdit (you can get that at Apples website) and paste the graphics from the gif over the graphics on the sheet.

Chains is not an easy scenario, but don't give up! Keep trying. If you have a lot of trouble, just email me. HINT: Nearly everyone talks in this scenario. If you can figure out what to do next, talk to people!
